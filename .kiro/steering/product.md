# Product Overview

Corporate website and landing page for **Jonas Westphal – IT & Cloud Solutions**, a freelance IT consulting business based in Germany.

## Purpose
Single-page marketing site showcasing IT services (n8n workflow automation, AI workflows, startup consulting, managed hosting) with a contact form for lead generation.

## Key Features
- Bilingual content: German (default) and English
- Dark/light theme toggle
- Contact form with Cloudflare Turnstile bot protection, rate limiting, and email delivery via Amazon SES
- Cookie consent banner with granular category control (necessary, analytics)
- SEO: Open Graph, Twitter Cards, hreflang tags, JSON-LD structured data, sitemap
- Responsive design with mobile hamburger menu

## Target Audience
Small-to-medium businesses and startups looking for IT infrastructure, automation, and hosting services.

## Domain
Production: `https://www.jonaswestphal.de` (German at root, English at `/en/`)
